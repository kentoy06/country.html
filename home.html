ABOUT BELGIUM

Belgium, officially known as the Kingdom of Belgium, is a small but culturally rich country in Western Europe. It is famous for its medieval towns, Renaissance architecture, and status as the heart of the European Union, with its capital, Brussels, hosting major EU institutions. Belgium is multilingual, with three official languages: Dutch (spoken in the Flemish region), French (in Wallonia), and German (in a smaller region near the German border). This diversity gives the country a unique blend of cultures and traditions.
